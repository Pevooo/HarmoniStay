This web application is a Hotel Management System designed with many features to help manage the hotel.  
The aim of this system is to provide an all in one system to help hotel managers manage their hotel or resort in an effecient way. 
HarmoniStay is a hotel management system designed to take hotel management to another level, designed by Pavly, Aya, John, Jessica.
